Shafaa Munjal


My name is Paula Flores. I was born in Los Angeles in 2002 and raised in Palm Desert.
I am a high school student and in my future, I plan to be an animator.		 I live in Palm Desert, California- home of 120 degree heat and the Coachella Festival.
My current education takes place in Palm Desert High School and
I am studying art, film, and computer science, as well as general education.		 As an artist, I tend to extend to different mediums to try them out.
My main choice is traditional art, pencil and paper, but recently, I have begun to work on some digital art.
I have also worked with mediums such as oil, acryllic, watercolor, and charcoal.
