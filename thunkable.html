The overall purpose of my program is to quiz the user’ trivia knowledge. The user selects a correct answer to the first question and the score goes up by 1 while a checkmark animation displays on the screen. The user then selects an incorrect answer to the second question and the score goes down by 1 while an x animation displays on the screen. The input is the user’s answer choice and the output is the score and the specific animation depending on whether the user’s answer choice was correct or incorrect.
